Project information

  • Category: Programming, Project Management

Project detail

This is my 3rd year university project that I have created with 4 other people.
For this project, I, as a Senior, worked as a Lead Programmer, Project Manager, and Co-leader along side with another Senior.

The final project features:

  • Procedural level generation using drunken walk algorithm.
  • Weapon attachments.
  • Shops.
  • Inventory.
  • AI.

Although this is not my first time working in a team, and even guiding a team, however, this is my first time ever officially led a team with a decent scale like this, therefore, I learned a lot in terms of both project management and programming.

Regarding programming, I were in charged of making and monitoring complex systems such as dungeon genartions, weapon attachments, and weapon system. I am mostly satisfied that I managed to create a dungeon generation system using the drunken walk algorithm. This while is a pretty complex system, but by successfully implemented it, I have improved the skills of algorithm application and problem solving.
