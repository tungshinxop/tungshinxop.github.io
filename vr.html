VR Mountain | University Project

Project information

  • Category: Programming
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Team size: 1 person
  • Tools: Unity, C#
  • Project URL: APK Download

Project detail

This is a Virtual Reality project that is made in Unity. For this, I was in charge of designing and developing my first ever VR game.

The final project features complex systems such as:

  • VR climbing with stamina system.
  • Rope swinging.
  • Boat rowing.

Since this is my first time ever working with VR, though I did have some problems to get my feet wet with VR development, but in the end, I learned many important skills regarding VR development, including:

  • Motion controller input setup.
  • XR setup with Unity.
  • Diegetic UI design and development
