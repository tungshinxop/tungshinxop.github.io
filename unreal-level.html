Unreal Tournament Level Design | University project

Project information

  • Category: Level design
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Team size: 1 person
  • Tools: Unreal Tournament, Photoshop, Magical Voxel
  • Project URL: Design Document

Project detail

In this project, I was tasked to create an upto 5v5 capture the flag level in Unreal Tournament. For this module, I learnt about the fundamentals of the levels design such as the process of creating a level, environment story-telling, and principles of level design.

This level went through the whole process of sketching, making pixel map, grey boxing and finally meshing. Throughout the process, I also tested the level extensively and responded to feedbacks for a better end product.
